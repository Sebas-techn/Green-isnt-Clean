What’s the Problem?

Tesco markets products as "eco-friendly" and "climate neutral" without robust third-party verification. According to the ASA and CMA, such unsubstantiated claims may violate the UK Green Claims Code and mislead consumers who want to make sustainable choices. Join us in demanding clarity, honesty, and accountability.
